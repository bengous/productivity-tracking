# 🧠 ADHD Productivity Blueprint

**Goal:** Transform "Time Blindness" into "Time Visibility" and reduce Executive Function friction.
*Reference: [ADHD Productivity Guide](https://super-productivity.com/guides/adhd-productivity/)*

## 1. The Core Friction Points
1.  **Time Blindness:** Not knowing how long things take or how much time has passed.
2.  **Task Paralysis:** The inability to start because the steps are unclear.
3.  **Transition Cost:** The high energy cost of switching tasks.

## 2. The Strategy: "Soft Boxing" & Buffers

Unlike rigid scheduling, **Soft Boxing** assumes you will fail to stick to the plan.

### ✅ The Rules
*   **Buffer Rule:** Always schedule **20-40% less** than you think you can do.
    *   *Example:* If you have 8 hours, plan for 5 hours of work.
*   **The "Panic Box":** Leave one hour completely empty. This is for the inevitable fire-drill or distraction.
*   **Visual Anchors:** Use the Progress Bar in Super Productivity. If it's not moving, you aren't working.

## 3. The "3-Step Transition Stack"

Use this when you feel stuck or overwhelmed:

1.  **Capture:** Write down the *one* thing blocking you (or the distraction). Get it out of your head.
2.  **Micro-Step:** Identify the absolute smallest next step.
    *   *Bad:* "Refactor API"
    *   *Good:* "Open api.ts file"
3.  **Timer Start:** Hit `Shift+X` (Start Timer). The ticking clock creates a "Body Doubling" effect.

## 4. Energy-Based Planning

Don't sort by priority; sort by **Cognitive Load**.

| Energy Level | Task Type | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🟢 **High** | **Deep Work** | Coding complex logic, Archtecture |
| 🟡 **Medium** | **Maintenance** | Code reviews, Documentation, Bug fixes |
| 🔴 **Low** | **Admin** | Emails, Slack, updating Jira |

> **Rule:** Never schedule High Energy tasks for the afternoon slump.
